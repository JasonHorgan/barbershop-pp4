Welcome to Barney's Barbershop

Barney's Barbershop, established in 1995 and located in the heart of Greystones, County Wicklow, is your go-to destination for timeless cuts, contemporary styles, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. With a long-standing tradition of quality grooming and personalized service, Barney's combines traditional barbering techniques with modern trends to offer a range of services, from classic haircuts to beard trims and styling. With nearly three decades of experience, our team of skilled barbers is dedicated to ensuring every customer leaves looking sharp and feeling their best.
